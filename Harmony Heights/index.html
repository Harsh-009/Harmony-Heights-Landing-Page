HARMONY HEIGHTS MUSIC ACADEMY:

Ascend to Artistry, Bound For
Broadway

Immerse yourself in the confluence of jazz, classical, rock, blues, and more through expert mentorship at Harmony Heights Music Academy. Our esteemed institution is dedicated to weaving an intricate and enriching fabric of musical education that is both comprehensive and rigorous. The academy is the brainchild of two distinguished musicians – Robert Ziegler, an accomplished jazz guitarist whose virtuosity extends to rock and blues, and James Sith, a classical piano savant, who is an alumnus of the illustrious Manhattan School of Music. Harmony Heights Music Academy stands as a sanctuary for individuals seeking to delve into and master the true essence of music.
